White Country Gravy

Description

This simple and delicious white gravy is a wonderful topping for biscuits, or hearty beef meals.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 stick of butter
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 2 cups milk (whole or 2%)
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 or 1 tbsp black pepper

Steps

  1. Melt the butter in a large saucepan
  2. Add the flour when you see small bubbles forming in the butter
  3. Whisk consistently until there is a toasty aroma and the mixture becomes golden (3-5 mins)
  4. Over time, add more and more of the milk (save about 1/4 cup)
  5. Add salt and pepper
  6. Wait until it is your desired consistency
  7. If not ready to serve, you can add the saved milk later to thin the gravy
