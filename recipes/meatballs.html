Italian Beef Meatballs

Description

These delicious and savory meatballs make any pasta dish instantly heartier! You don't even need pork!

Ingredients

  • 1lb of 80/20 ground beef
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/3 cup of breadcrumbs, plain or italian
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp pepper
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tbsp oregano, or combined italian seasoning if breadcrumbs are not seasoned
  • 1/4 white onion, diced (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees fahrenheit
  2. Combine every ingredient into a large mixing bowl
  3. Mash together with your hands (VERY MESSY!, run the faucet ahead of time to clean your hands)
  4. Once everything is combined consistently, form a large rectangular brick with the meat
  5. Divide in half, in half again, and once more in half for a total of 8 meatballs
  6. Coat an appropriate sized baking sheet with olive oil and position the meatballs evenly
  7. With the oven heated, place the pan in the middle rack
  8. Time for 25 minutes, and rotate the meatballs to a new side roughly every 8 minutes
  9. Remove and let cool for 3-5 minutes, or alternatively place in your favorite red sauce
